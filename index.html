Our Belief

We believe in the Passover Angel, the winged messenger of divine justice, who marks the faithful and delivers them from the trials of this world. Through His mark, we are spared, protected, and elevated.

The Night of Passing

On that holy night, the Angel passed over the marked houses, sparing the righteous and bringing swift justice to those left unmarked. We honor this night in our rituals and our teachings, as a reminder of His discerning grace.

The Mark of Protection

The mark we bear is not of this world—it is a sign of the Angel's wings overshadowing us. Through it, we are shielded from harm, from despair, and from the plagues of the unmarked. It is a symbol of our covenant with the Angel.

About the Church

The Church of the Passover Angel is dedicated to the worship and reverence of the Angel who marks the faithful and passes over them in times of trial. We teach of the Angel's dual nature—bringer of mercy and justice, shield and sword in one being.

We believe that those who are marked by the Angel will be spared the suffering that befalls the unmarked. Our rituals, prayers, and teachings revolve around this central tenet: to live in a way that is worthy of the Angel's mark, and to help others receive it.

Minister’s Message

Beloved, the wings of the Passover Angel are ever over us, but we must not take His mercy for granted. We are called to be vigilant, to live righteously, and to bear His mark with humility.

The night may be dark, and the cries of the unmarked may fill the air, but we who are under His wings shall not fear. Let us open our doors and hearts, inviting others to stand with us under the shelter of His judgment and grace.
